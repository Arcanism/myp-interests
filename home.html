Sports

Sports are forms of physical activity and games which people play to have fun or watch others play for entertainment. Sports are often a part of every school, they are integrated into the daily activities of many children who play sports at their school, at their house and are also a form of social interaction with other people. It is also one of the biggest forms of entertainment, where professionals at a sport play with and against each other, representing their country at times, while people from all over the world watch.
Popular sports include: Athletics; Football; Basketball; Cricket; Tennis; Badminton; Hockey; Table Tennis; Volleyball; American Football; Baseball; Golf; Rugby and many more.

Books

Through books, various forms of literature are given out to readers of all types. Books can be fictional novels, real stories, facts/information and many more, as the content for books are limitless and only bound by the creativity of the author. This section can be about books you have read and liked, a book that you have created or even discuss about stories you have seen.

Music

Music is a form of art involving the composition of sound to convey emotions, ideas or even simply please a person. Music can compose of vocals, instrumentals or digitally produced sounds and most often a combination of these. Music is used almost everywhere in the form of songs, soundtracks or background music in various genres. The lyrics of a song can convey emotions and messages from the artist to an audience and the rhythm of any soundtrack can set a mood for the listener. You can talk about your favorite songs, artists, soundtracks or even about the more technical aspects of music such as the composition of it.

Gaming

Gaming is a form of virtual entertainment where people interact with an interface to achieve something and move forward. Games can be played without another person or even with multiple other people as multiplayer games. They are a very popular form of entertainment and most kids in the current generation play video games. If you are interested in finding other people to play with or get recommendations from others and even find tips to playing games, you can know more here!

Tech

Tech, a short form for technology, is a popular interest regarding various hardware devices such as computers, phones, gaming consoles and accessories. Various information about hardware systems and applications are widely discussed and are important when deciding a device to buy. Many people who are interested in technology are highly knowledgeable about how the technical specifications relate to the performance and application of the device. If you are interested in exploring how devices work and which devices are better for your use, technology is the perfect interest for you
