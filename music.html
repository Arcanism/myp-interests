Music
Songs

Songs are a composition of lyrics and notes, to convey an emotion or a message through the lyrics and set a theme with a song. This is one of the most popular forms of music, artists around the world compose millions of songs in various genres like pop, rap, rock, metal and jazz. All these genres portray different aspects of cultures, it can convey different types of emotions and convey various meanings. 90% of the global population listen to music, and most of them listen to songs.

Soundtracks

Soundtracks are a composition of only notes and instrumentals, used to set a mood with the help of its rhythm and tempo. They are most often used to support different types of media, used in films, drama, games, songs and as a background music. Soundtracks are created using both instruments and digital notes, soundtracks are the most used forms of music as all types of music require a soundtrack. They are often used to relax a person and withdraw themselves from the real world.

Instruments

Musical instruments are devices used to create sounds in different pitches and rhythms in a certain pattern to create a flow of notes in order to create music. They are often practiced as hobbies by many people to learn how to play an instruments, and certain people pursue it to become professionals in playing the instrument. Various instruments are played together to create more complex forms of music.

Guide to using the chatbox

Click on the "name" input box to set your username (at least 2 characters: A-Z and 0-9), it can be changed anytime and is temporary. DO NOT IMPERSONATE ANYONE!

Click on the icon to the top left of the name input box to change your avatar (profile picture)
Insert a URL or upload a file, DO NOT USE ANY NSFW OR OFFENSIVE IMAGES

Click on the message input box to type your message and click the send button at the right or press the enter key to send the message.
Do not write any offensive or nsfw content

Click on the emoji button to the left of the message box to insert emojis into your message
It will appear as an emoticon in the message box itself, typing the emoticon will send as an emoji too.

The date/time when the message was sent is written towards the right of the message
The username of the person who sent the message is on the left
You can link a person or specify them by clicking on their name when sending your message to include the tag
