Gaming
Multiplayer (Online)

Multiplayer games are online games where people play with others through the internet and either attempt to achieve a goal together by coordinating with each other or playing against one another to compare their skill. These are the most popular forms of games as they are dynamic and the situations keep changing based on the other person. Games are usually played by a younger audience but there is a large population of an older audience who play them too, there are over 2.5 billion people who play multiplayer games and there are many forms of interactions which take place through games, allowing people to build relationships.

Single player (Online and offline)

Single player games can be played both online and offline, it involves the user playing without anyone else and competing against the computer to achieve their goal. They gradually process through the game and try to do various things to finish the game. People across the world often compete for different records to set within the game, these types of games appeal to all ages equally. Although they are not as popular as multiplayer games, they are known for their difficulty and require a lot of time to complete. They give the player a sense of satisfaction when they achieve something in the game.

Guide to using the chatbox

Click on the "name" input box to set your username (at least 2 characters: A-Z and 0-9), it can be changed anytime and is temporary. DO NOT IMPERSONATE ANYONE!

Click on the icon to the top left of the name input box to change your avatar (profile picture)
Insert a URL or upload a file, DO NOT USE ANY NSFW OR OFFENSIVE IMAGES

Click on the message input box to type your message and click the send button at the right or press the enter key to send the message.
Do not write any offensive or nsfw content

Click on the emoji button to the left of the message box to insert emojis into your message
It will appear as an emoticon in the message box itself, typing the emoticon will send as an emoji too.

The date/time when the message was sent is written towards the right of the message
The username of the person who sent the message is on the left
You can link a person or specify them by clicking on their name when sending your message to include the tag
