Books
Fiction

Fictional books are stories and novels written based on the imagination of the author. They are often set in places that do not exist and show imaginary concepts, this includes genres like fantasy, horror and sci fi. They are one of the most popular types of books, typically appealing to a younger audience who are fascinated by the imaginary concepts and is also a method for them to escape reality.

Realistic fiction

Realistic fiction covers fictional stories that are set in a real setting where the story is the only fictional concept. These types of books are typically written to show the life of a character or a story to inspire/display. This often includes historical fiction where the real events that happened in the past are modified to have stories that are imagined by the author, an hypothetical situation within the past.

Non-fiction

Non fiction books are real life events and real stories which happened somewhere, they are often in forms of biographies or stories to inform the reader about what happened. This can also include informative books about how the world works, like sciences and stories of people who have gone through various events. The most common non fiction books are historical books, showing what happened in the past and describing the events that happened long ago.

Guide to using the chatbox

Click on the "name" input box to set your username (at least 2 characters: A-Z and 0-9), it can be changed anytime and is temporary. DO NOT IMPERSONATE ANYONE!

Click on the icon to the top left of the name input box to change your avatar (profile picture)
Insert a URL or upload a file, DO NOT USE ANY NSFW OR OFFENSIVE IMAGES

Click on the message input box to type your message and click the send button at the right or press the enter key to send the message.
Do not write any offensive or nsfw content

Click on the emoji button to the left of the message box to insert emojis into your message
It will appear as an emoticon in the message box itself, typing the emoticon will send as an emoji too.

The date/time when the message was sent is written towards the right of the message
The username of the person who sent the message is on the left
You can link a person or specify them by clicking on their name when sending your message to include the tag

Please follow the guidelines given in the footer to protect your privacy and protect your personal information
