Football

Football is a team sport where 11 people in a team strategically take up different positions, playing against another team with the objective to send the football into the opponent's goal post. It is one of the most popular sports, people around the world play it in different ways and is played by professionals to entertain audiences, representing countries, cities and various other teams. Football is the most watched sport on telivision with over 3 billion viewers. Football is highly reliant on your atheltic skills, your ability to run and also your skill in dribbling the ball and shooting it.

The professional sport is overseen by FIFA, they handle multiple leagues like the world cup, the UEFA, La Liga, Premier League and many more.

Basketball

Basketball is another team sport, with a team of 5 people playing against another team to get the ball into the basket on the opponent's side. It is quite a popular sport, professional basketball is mostly played in the United States, representing different states. The sport focuses majorly on the skill of accuracy, shooting the ball into the basketball from varying distances with accuracy and handling the ball with your hands without losing it.

Cricket

Cricket is a team sport, one of the most popular, and people often play it as a fun sport in many countries. It is one of the most popular sports with over 2.5 billion viewers, and has full capacity audiences in stadiums during international and IPL matches. Cricket originates from england, it is called the gentleman's game due to the game evolving to be a sport where the players are highly respectful. It can also be played almost anywhere as long as you have a bat and a ball, making it a very flexible game for having fun.

Tennis

Tennis is a sport played between 2 people or 2 against 2. You have to hit the ball over the net and within the boundaries before it bounces twice with your bat. It is reliant on your arm power and reflexes as you need to react quickly and position yourself properly to hit it in the correct angle and get it to the other side. It is the most popular one on one sport, it improves your hand eye coordination, is a good excercise and hobby and also improves your reflexes.
